Where Do Manta Rays Live?

Manta rays are native to oceans in tropical and subtropical climates. They inhabit temperate, tropical, and subtropical waters around the globe, including parts of the Indian Ocean, Atlantic Ocean, and Pacific Ocean. They typically live in shallow lagoons, coral reefs, estuaries, and coastal areas. Manta rays prefer living in warm waters with ample food sources.
