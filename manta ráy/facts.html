Interesting Facts

  • Manta rays can have a wingspan of up to 7 meters (23 feet).
  • They are filter feeders, eating mainly plankton and small fish.
  • Manta rays are highly intelligent and have the largest brain-to-body ratio of any fish.
  • They are harmless to humans and often interact playfully with divers.
  • Each manta ray has a unique spot pattern on its belly, like a fingerprint.
